# Angle Protocol

A tiny browser-based, split-screen, first-person tactical shooter for two people sharing one keyboard.

## Verified goals

- HTML/Canvas weekend toy.
- Retro raycaster first-person view with a more dimensional 3D-style scene, weapon viewmodel, perspective floor, and depth shading.
- Split-screen local play.
- Serious tactical feel.
- 2v2 elimination: Player 1 plus bot versus Player 2 plus bot.
- No economy, no spike mode, no copied Valorant names, maps, agents, art, layouts, or sounds.
- Version 1 has AK-47 primary fire, pistol secondary fire, visible projectiles, ammo, reloads, headshot-style precision damage, bots, a 2-minute round timer, lethals, and flashes.

## Client screens

- Loading screen opens first, then moves into the game client.
- Home shows the current season directive, queue readiness, update cards, and a direct Play button.
- Play has Local 2v2 and Single Player practice modes, plus a client-side map vote.
- Squad lets you pick an original agent, callsign, squad motto, and season tone.
- Loadout lets you choose a primary training preset and shows the active sidearm skin.
- Contracts lets you pick a progression focus.
- Profile tracks simple session stats and recent round history.
- Collection lets you equip an original sidearm skin used by Player 1 in-match.
- Settings changes round length, bot difficulty, visual quality, and shows keybinds.

## Controls

Player 1:

- `W`: forward
- `S`: backward
- `A`: turn left
- `D`: turn right
- `Q`: shoot
- `E`: reload
- `Shift`: sprint
- `F`: jump
- `1`: AK-47
- `2`: pistol
- `3`: lethal
- `4`: flash

Player 2:

- `P`: forward
- `;`: backward
- `L`: turn left
- `'`: turn right
- `O`: shoot
- `[`: reload
- `/` or `?`: sprint
- `.`: jump
- `8`: rifle
- `9`: pistol
- `0`: lethal
- `-`: flash

The game starts the next round automatically after a short pause.

Press `Escape` or the match `Exit` button to return to the client.
